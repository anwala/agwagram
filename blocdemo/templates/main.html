B3T (BLOC-Based Behavioural Tool) is an online utility to help in analyzing and quantitatively describing the behaviour of Twitter accounts. In an age of growing misinformation, B3T can assist in identifying likely bot accounts as well as tracking shifts in their behaviour.

While BLOC can be applied to other social media accounts, this tool currently supports Twitter exclusively.
